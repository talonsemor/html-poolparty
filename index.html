    1.Requirments

    Here are some common requirements for pool party guests to help keep the event fun, safe, and organized:

    Basic Requirements

    • Swimwear: Guests should bring appropriate swimsuits, cover-ups, and extra towels.
    • Footwear: Flip-flops or waterproof sandals are best for around the pool.
    • Sunscreen: Everyone should bring (or use provided) sunscreen to prevent sunburn.
    • Change of clothes: A dry outfit for after swimming.

    Etiquette & Safety

    • No glass items near the pool — only plastic or metal cups and bottles.
    • Know how to swim or stay in shallow areas if not confident.
    • Follow pool rules — no diving in shallow water, no running around the pool, etc.
    • Supervise children — an adult should always watch kids in or near water.

    Optional but Helpful

    • Bring a pool float or inflatable (if allowed).
    • A hat or sunglasses* for sun protection.
    • BYOB or snack contribution,if the host requests it.
    • Positive vibes only— come ready to relax, dance, and have fun!
